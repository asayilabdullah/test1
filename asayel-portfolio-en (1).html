03Selected Work

Most of my work sits at the intersection of finance and everyday habits — redesigning onboarding flows for a personal finance app so new users could link an account without giving up halfway, and rebuilding a cluttered invoicing dashboard into a clean design system a whole product team could actually use without filing a support ticket every week.

More recently I worked on a booking platform for a therapy practice, where the real challenge wasn't the visuals at all — it was cutting a nervous, six-step booking process down to something someone could get through in one sitting, on a day when getting through it at all was the hard part.
